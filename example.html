About the Project

Networking the Revolution (1688-1692) focuses on the Study of letters exchanged during key historical moments in Scotland. Through computational data science methods like network science and prosopography, this project sheds light on the relationships and key players of the time.

Explore the connections, the impact of these letters, and the historical nuances of Scotland's past.
